# Primary User Personas

Status: Draft  
Version: MVP-001

## Persona 1: Gongfu Practitioner

- Goal: Conduct focused tea sessions with accurate timing and repeatable parameters.
- Pain points: Forgets steep timing, temperature, tea amount, and which session worked well.
- MVP needs: Brew timer, multi-steep recipes, session history, ability to repeat successful sessions.
- Success signal: Can start and complete a ceremony without switching tools.

## Persona 2: Tea Collector

- Goal: Maintain a clear view of owned teas and remaining quantities.
- Pain points: Forgets what is available, loses track of stock, lacks structured notes.
- MVP needs: Tea catalog, stock tracking, favorites, availability status, tea type and origin fields.
- Success signal: Can decide what to drink from current inventory.

## Persona 3: Teaware Collector

- Goal: Track teaware by purpose, material, volume, and suitable brewing methods.
- Pain points: Owns many pieces but lacks a structured way to pair them with tea sessions.
- MVP needs: Teaware shelf, material and volume fields, purpose, supported brewing methods.
- Success signal: Can choose suitable teaware while planning a session.

## Persona 4: Beginner Tea Enthusiast

- Goal: Start learning Chinese tea through structured practice.
- Pain points: Does not know taxonomy, equipment purpose, or suitable brewing parameters.
- MVP needs: Simple required fields, useful defaults, calm empty states, clear labels.
- Success signal: Can add first tea, add first teaware item, and run a guided timer.

## Future Persona: Advanced Aging and Tasting User

- Goal: Track aging, tasting evolution, advanced notes, and AI-assisted recommendations.
- MVP treatment: Deferred. Do not add aging analytics or AI fields to MVP specs.
