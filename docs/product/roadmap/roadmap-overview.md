# Product Roadmap

Status: Approved for Implementation
Version: MVP-001

## Phase 1: MVP

Goal: Users can catalog tea and teaware while conducting guided tea sessions.

Included:

- Tea Shelf
- Teaware Shelf
- Brew Timer
- Session history
- Favorites
- Remaining stock tracking
- Tea taxonomy fields required for inventory organization

## Phase 2: Accounts and Sync

Goal: Preserve user collections across devices.

Candidate scope:

- Registration and login
- Cloud sync
- Multi-device access
- User profile
- Language settings

## Phase 3: Knowledge Shelf

Goal: Add learning support after core practice workflows are proven.

Candidate scope:

- Articles
- Beginner guides
- Tea types, regions, and processing methods
- Non-AI recommendations

## Phase 4: AI Tea Assistant

Goal: Provide personalized brewing, mood, teaware, and recipe guidance.

Candidate scope:

- Brewing recommendations
- Mood-based tea suggestions
- Recipe optimization
- Beginner assistance

## Phase 5: Monetization

Goal: Introduce revenue after retention and product value are validated.

Candidate scope:

- Premium AI recommendations
- Advanced inventory and aging analytics
- Cloud backup
- Exclusive guides
- Marketplace or affiliate tea stores

## Roadmap Guardrails

- Do not start Phase 2 until MVP collection and timer workflows are accepted.
- Do not start AI before there is enough user-owned tea, teaware, recipe, and session context to make recommendations meaningful.
- Do not introduce monetization until core retention is validated.
