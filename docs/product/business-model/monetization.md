# Monetization

Status: Future Assumptions  
Version: MVP-001

## MVP Position

Monetization is excluded from MVP. The MVP must focus on proving practical user value through tea cataloging, teaware cataloging, and guided sessions.

## Future Opportunities

- Premium AI brewing recommendations
- Advanced inventory analytics
- Tea aging analytics
- Cloud backup and sync
- Exclusive learning guides
- Marketplace partnerships
- Affiliate tea stores

## Commercial Assumptions

- Assumption: Users will pay only after Tea Shelf becomes a trusted record of their collection and practice.
  Impact if wrong: Monetization timing and packaging may need validation earlier.

- Assumption: AI and cloud backup are stronger premium candidates than basic cataloging.
  Impact if wrong: Free/paid boundaries may require product research before Phase 5.
