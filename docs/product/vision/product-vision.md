# Tea Shelf Product Vision

Status: Approved for Implementation
Version: MVP-001  
Audience: Product, design, engineering, QA, and delivery agents

## Product Mission

Tea Shelf / Чайная Полка helps Chinese tea enthusiasts organize, improve, and deepen their personal tea practice.

The product is a personal tea ceremony assistant, not only a catalog. It combines collection management, teaware awareness, brewing guidance, and session memory so users can conduct tea practice with less friction and more consistency.

## MVP Promise

Users can catalog tea and teaware while conducting guided tea sessions.

## Product Principles

- Protect ritual focus: core flows must feel calm, focused, and low-friction.
- Keep MVP narrow: Tea Shelf, Teaware Shelf, and Brew Timer are the only MVP modules.
- Preserve future extensibility: future AI, knowledge, sync, mobile, and monetization work must not distort MVP scope.
- Treat personal collection data as user-owned and privacy-sensitive.
- Prefer simple, inspectable workflows over opaque automation.

## MVP Modules

- Tea Shelf: personal tea inventory with type, origin, stock, notes, favorites, and availability.
- Teaware Shelf: teaware inventory with category, material, volume, purpose, and brewing method support.
- Brew Timer: guided multi-steep sessions, recipe timing, temperature notes, and session history.

## Deferred Modules

- Knowledge Shelf
- AI Tea Assistant
- Authentication and cloud sync, unless approved as MVP+
- Native mobile applications
- Monetization
- Social, marketplace, and community features
