# Mission

Tea Shelf exists to help modern tea lovers maintain a living record of their tea practice.

The product should help users answer three practical questions:

- What tea and teaware do I currently own?
- How should I brew this tea with the teaware available to me?
- What happened during the last ceremony, and how can I repeat or improve it?

For MVP, the mission is satisfied by collection management and guided brewing sessions. Education, AI, accounts, and commerce remain future expansion areas.
