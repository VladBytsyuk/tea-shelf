# MVP Market Assumptions

Status: Assumptions  
Version: MVP-001

## Audience Assumptions

- Chinese tea enthusiasts need tea-specific collection and session tooling.
- Beginners benefit from structured practice before formal education content.
- Collectors value inventory accuracy and notes.
- Gongfu practitioners value repeatable timing and session memory.

## Competitive Assumptions

- General note-taking tools are flexible but not tea-specific.
- Generic inventory apps do not support brewing context.
- Timer apps do not preserve tea, teaware, and session relationships.
- Tea education sites provide content but not personal practice management.

## Validation Needed

- Interview tea enthusiasts about current tracking habits.
- Compare existing tea apps, inventory apps, and timer apps.
- Validate willingness to maintain structured inventory.
- Validate whether session history drives repeat usage.

## MVP Implication

The MVP should prove that integrated inventory plus guided brewing is more useful than separate notes and timer tools.
